About

Pathirikkunnatth Mana at Palakkat dist.of old Valluvanaat area at Mundakkottukurishi which lies between Cherplachery and Shornur, is the major snake worshipping centre.This family is also known as Kulappuratthu mana. It is calculated that this family's snake worshipping tradition dates back about 1500 years. long long back the couple in the family were not having any children and were very much sad They were scared that the identity of the family will be lost forever because of that. So they prayed the village diety Lord Ayyappa requesting him to find a solution to their sorrow. Dharmma Shaastha told them to worship Vatakkum Nathan of Thrisshivaperoor. The Nampoothiri woorshipped VatakkumNathan with austerity and one daya the Lord was pleased and appeared before him and told him that his wish was granted and he can go back to his home. At home both of them continued the worship and finally the Nampoothiri lady was blessed with a boy. At the time of delivery she gave birth to a snake too. Both grew together and whereever the boy went, the snake too accompanied him. The relatives and their neighbours were scared of visiting them. So the parents told the snake, 'seeing you here every where, many are frightened to visit us'. Father said it ina raised voice and mother supported father. The snake was sad and replied that I am going to disappear and won"t be seen inside the house any were but I want Amma"s 'Nivedyam' or offer me cooked rice every day Amma agreed to that and the snake blessed them saying that this house will be a protector for those who are cursed by snakes and will become famouse for treatment or...... Saying that the snake disappeared inbehind a stone in a corner of the raised hall at the northern part of the house. This isthe retold story of the family diety

F.A.Q

  • When will the Temple be open ?

    Temple will be open to everyone on Early morning 6:00 AM.

  • Is there any booking required here for Darshan?

    No, You don't need anytype of advance booking. everyone can get a free darshan.

  • Is there any dress code for entering here?

    Yes, Mens should remove thier shirts at the time of darshan. Else everytime there is no restrictions.

  • What kind of Offerings are available?

    Sarppabali is the most popular offerings that are done here.

  • Is there any fixed cost for Sarppabali?

    There is a fixed charge for this offering, because this is somewhat a big pooja.

  • When will the temple close?

    Devottees are allowed for darshan untill 6:00 PM . After that no one is permitted inside.
